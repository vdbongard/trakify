About

Trakify is about tracking episodes of tv shows that you have seen. But it also allows to discover trending shows, create a watchlist for shows that you want to see and statistics of shows and episodes that you have already seen.

It uses Trakt under the hood. That means you have to connect your Trakt account if you have one or create a new account. This happens automatically when clicking on the login button of the login page where you get redirected to the Trakt page.
