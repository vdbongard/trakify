About

Trakify is about tracking episodes of TV shows that you have seen. It also allows to discover trending shows, create a watchlist for shows that you want to see and statistics about what you have already seen.

It uses Trakt under the hood. This means you have to connect your Trakt account if you have one or create a new Trakt account. The login button of the login page redirects you to the Trakt page to log in or sign up with Trakt.
